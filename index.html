Beach Haven, Long Beach Island

Beach Haven, LBI

Room blocks are available at two hotels under the Schumann – Haber reservation at the Drifting Sands Hotel and Holiday Inn Manahawkin. Transportation will be provided from both hotels and more information will be provided upon check in. We also have two additional non-block hotel recommendations that are oceanfront and located in the heart of Beach Haven. Both are very close to the venue! More information about each hotel and additional non-block accommodations are listed below for your reference. Please feel free to reach out if you have any questions when booking your stay!

We would love our guests to stay for a long weekend and enjoy all the things we love about Beach Haven and Long Beach Island! We highly recommend renting a house on the island through Diane Turton realtors or online booking through AirBnB, HomeAway or VRBO since September is a beautiful month to rent at the beach. Although September is also considered “off-season”, the island is a popular area year-round and houses do book up quickly, so don’t wait, book now! We recommend staying in Beach Haven (or very close to it) as the ceremony and reception will be held here and many of our recommended and favorite things to do are in this area. We look forward to seeing everyone on the island!

Getting Around

Not only is Beach Haven the heart of LBI. It has many of our favorite places and things within walking distance from each other! But some places may require some longer travel. Here are some of the services we use to get around the island.

Taxi & Shuttle Services


Mike’s Affordable Shuttle Service

Long Beach Island, NJ
(609) 287-3028
http://www.mikesaffordableshuttle.com/

Uber and Lyft are limited on the island, we recommend this local taxi service just in case!

VISIT SITE

LBI Shuttle

Long Beach Island, NJ
(609) 342-2111
https://www.lbishuttle.com/

Long Beach Island Shuttles are a shuttle service that is run along all 18 miles of the island. With designated areas to stop it is easy to find a trolley and waive them down to get anywhere you need.

Shuttle Fees:

  • $2 per ride 10am - 7pm.
  • $5 per ride 7pm - close
  • $125 season pass (all times)
