Where to Stay

Where to Stay

Room blocks are available at Drifting Sands Hotel and Holiday Inn Manahawkin under the Schumann – Haber reservation. Transportation will be provided from both hotels.

We also have two additional non-block hotel recommendations that are oceanfront and located in the heart of Beach Haven. Both are very close to the venue! More information below.

Lastly, we would love our guests to stay for a long weekend and enjoy all the things we love about Beach Haven and Long Beach Island! We highly recommend renting a house on the island through Diane Turton realtors or online booking through AirBnB, VRBO, or the other online rental sites listed since September is a beautiful month to rent at the beach. Although September is also considered “off-season”, the island is a popular area year-round and houses do book up quickly, so don’t wait, book now! We recommend staying in Beach Haven (or very close to it) as the ceremony and reception will be held here and many of our recommended and favorite things to do are in this area. We look forward to seeing everyone on the island!

Blocked Hotels


Drifting Sands Oceanfront Hotel

119 East 9th Street, Ship Bottom, NJ 08008
(877) 524-7866
Located on island 15 minutes north from venue

Please call the hotel directly to make a reservation under the Schumann – Haber wedding in order to get the group rates for the Ocean Side block.

All reservations must be made on or before the cut-off date 8/25/2021 or the rooms get released to the public. The Ocean Side rooms include a king bed or double beds, please request your preference upon booking. Additional room options at the hotel are available. The front desk is open 9am-5pm, 7 days a week during the high season from April 1st through mid-October. If booking during the off season, please leave a voicemail as the desk will call you back to assist with the reservation within a week.

Getting Around

One of the many reasons why we love Beach Haven is that it's in the heart of everything we love on the island and easily walkable and bikeable to our favorite spots! Below are a couple bike rental places we frequently rent from and taxi services if needed!

Taxi & Shuttle Services


Mike’s Affordable Shuttle Service

Long Beach Island, NJ
(609) 287-3028

Uber and Lyft are limited on the island, we recommend this local taxi service just in case!

VISIT SITE

LBI Shuttle

Long Beach Island, NJ
(609) 342-2111

Long Beach Island Shuttles are a shuttle service that is run along all 18 miles of the island. With designated areas to stop it is easy to find a trolley and waive them down to get anywhere you need.

Shuttle Fees:

  • $2 per ride 10am - 7pm.
  • $5 per ride 7pm - close
  • $125 season pass (all times)
